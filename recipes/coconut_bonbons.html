Coconut Bonbons

Description

These cute coconut bonbons are little balls of joy. Perfect for gifting or just treating yourself!

Ingredients

  1. 1/4 cup butter
  2. 1 pound confectioners' sugar
  3. 1 cup sweetened condensed milk
  4. 2 cups flaked coconut
  5. 9 (1-ounce) squares semisweet chocolate
  6. 2 tablespoons shortening
